Phoenix City Hotel

Star: five-star Hotel

Address: one of Hengfu Yuexiu District No. 117, Guangzhou City, Guangdong Province,Guangzhou

As a Platina 5-star hotel, Phoenix City Hotel is one of the most luxurious hotel in Guangzhou and might be one of the most beautiful in China . The hotel is nestled in the est of 200 000 sq.m of greenery. The grand lobby is really astonishing. The Phoenix City Hotel is close to the city center yet to nature. Though it is not in the city center, the hotel has easy access to major highway network. It is about 20 minutes drive to the Canton Fair Pazhou Complex and 30 minutes to Canton Fair Liuhua Complex. The Guangzhou New Baiyun Airport has direct shuttle bus to the Hotel. The Hotel successy combine the advantages of modern luxury with the charm and elegance of authentic furnishing and fittings.The hotel boasts its first-class accommodation facilities. Each room is a showpiece of utmost comfort and elegance. Cozy rooms with courteous service causes the feeling of a home away from home. The hotel has the biggest conference and banquet facilities in Guangzhou , and various recreation facilities including shuffleboard room, tennis, swimming pool, and very posh & comfortable sauna center with spacious pools, steam rooms, ice rooms and complimentary snack, drinks, fruits. Come and stay at Phoenix City Hotel, experience the old world imperial excellence which meets with a new world of hospitality and service.
